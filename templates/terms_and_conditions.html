Terms and Conditions

Participation Rules

  1. Participants must register on EvalAI and accept these terms and conditions
  2. Teams can have a maximum of 4 members
  3. Multiple submissions from the same team must use different approaches
  4. The use of external data for training is allowed, but must be clearly documented

Code of Conduct

  • Participants must not attempt to reverse engineer the test set
  • Sharing of predictions or test set information between teams is prohibited
  • All submissions must be the original work of the participating team

Publication Policy

  • Participants retain the rights to their submissions and approaches
  • Top-performing teams are encouraged to share their approaches in the workshop
  • The organizers reserve the right to analyze and publish aggregate results

Timeline Compliance

  • All deadlines must be strictly followed
  • Late submissions will not be accepted
  • The organizers reserve the right to modify the timeline if necessary
